Privacy Policy for Strawberry Remote

Last updated: July 2026

Strawberry Remote is a network remote control for the Strawberry Music Player. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

Strawberry Remote stores only the IP address and port number of your Strawberry Music Player locally on your device. This information is used solely to establish a connection to your player.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device in private app storage. No data is uploaded to any server or cloud service.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The app communicates only with your own Strawberry Music Player on your local network.

Data Deletion

All locally stored data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. No data remains on any external server after uninstallation.

Network Access

The app requires access to your local network solely to communicate with the Strawberry Music Player. No internet connection is required or used by this app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us at: nwremote@zudiewiener.com
